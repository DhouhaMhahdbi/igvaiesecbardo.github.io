About our IGV team

The Incoming Global Volunteer is a part of the Local Committee Bardo in Tunisia. We manage the exchange participant, who are willing to change their lives, boost their confidence and develop their social skills through a cross cultural exchange program. “We strive to achieve peace and fulfillment of mankind’s potential”. This is our fundamental goal. We aim to create a peaceful world through attaining our SDGs (sustainable development goals); 17 steps denounced by the UN (United Nations) to solve all the global issues.

We are and proud to be one of the believers in the change and improvement of our country and the whole world through the AIESEC experience.

About Tunisia





Visiting a new country is an amazing adventure but Interning in a new country is more amazing. Yet, not being aware of the culture of the new country, its daily life and its living cost might be tricky. AIESEC Bardo is here to help! Because we CARE for the well-being of our EPs , we present you the living cost in Tunisia, to help alleviate any ambiguity on what to spend every day in your beautiful journey in our beloved country.












Although it’s famous for its sprawling sand dunes, elegant spas and desert Star War sets, the smallest country in North Africa is not always synonymous with beauty, but of course beauty is subjective and vastly underrated Tunisia is home to many places easily worthy of the word. From magical mountain oases to atmospheric holy cites.
